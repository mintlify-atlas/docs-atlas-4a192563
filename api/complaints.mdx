## Endpoint

```
POST /api/complaints
```

Submits a consumer complaint or claim through the electronic complaints book (Libro de Reclamaciones). This endpoint complies with Peruvian consumer protection law (Ley N° 29571) and INDECOPI requirements.

## Request Body

### Consumer Information

<ParamField body="name" type="string" required>
  Full legal name of the consumer
  
  **Validation:** Minimum 2 characters, maximum 200 characters
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="documentType" type="enum" required>
  Type of identification document
  
  **Allowed values:** `"DNI"`, `"CE"`, `"PASSPORT"`
  
  - **DNI**: Documento Nacional de Identidad (Peru)
  - **CE**: Carné de Extranjería
  - **PASSPORT**: International passport
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="documentNumber" type="string" required>
  Identification document number
  
  **Validation:** Minimum 4 characters, maximum 30 characters
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="address" type="string" required>
  Physical address of the consumer
  
  **Validation:** Minimum 5 characters, maximum 500 characters
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="phone" type="string" required>
  Contact phone number
  
  **Validation:** Minimum 6 characters, maximum 30 characters
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="email" type="string" required>
  Valid email address
  
  **Validation:** Must be a valid email format, maximum 200 characters
</ParamField>

### Minor Guardian Information

<ParamField body="isMinor" type="boolean">
  Indicates if the consumer is a minor (under 18)
  
  **Default:** `false`
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="guardianName" type="string">
  Full name of legal guardian or representative (required if `isMinor` is `true`)
  
  **Validation:** Maximum 200 characters
  
  **Default:** Empty string
</ParamField>

### Complaint Details

<ParamField body="type" type="enum" required>
  Type of consumer report
  
  **Allowed values:** `"reclamo"`, `"queja"`
  
  - **reclamo** (Claim): Complaint related to products or services
  - **queja** (Grievance): Complaint about customer service or attention
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="serviceDescription" type="string" required>
  Description of the product or service being disputed
  
  **Validation:** Minimum 10 characters, maximum 5000 characters
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="amountClaimed" type="number">
  Monetary amount being claimed (optional, only for "reclamo")
  
  **Validation:** Must be a positive number
  
  **Format:** Decimal with up to 2 decimal places
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="detail" type="string" required>
  Detailed explanation of the complaint or claim
  
  **Validation:** Minimum 10 characters, maximum 5000 characters
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="consumerRequest" type="string" required>
  What the consumer is requesting or expecting as resolution
  
  **Validation:** Minimum 10 characters, maximum 5000 characters
</ParamField>

## Response

<ResponseField name="message" type="string">
  Success confirmation message
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="id" type="integer">
  Unique complaint ID for tracking purposes
</ResponseField>

## Legal Compliance

### INDECOPI Requirements

This endpoint implements the electronic complaints book as required by:

- **Código de Protección y Defensa del Consumidor (Ley N° 29571)**
- **Decreto Supremo N° 011-2011-PCM**

### Response Timeline

According to Peruvian law, the company must respond to all complaints within **30 calendar days**. The confirmation email sent to the consumer includes this information.

### Company Information

All confirmation emails include the provider's legal information:
- **Company:** Enigma Developers S.A.C.
- **RUC:** 20605632875

## Behavior

When a complaint is successfully submitted:

1. **Database Record**: Creates a new complaint with `status: "pending"` and `read: false`
2. **Admin Notification**: Sends detailed email to company with all complaint information and unique ID
3. **User Confirmation**: Sends legal confirmation email to consumer with:
   - Unique complaint ID for tracking
   - 30-day response timeline
   - Company legal information
4. **Email Subject Format**: Uses pattern "Libro de Reclamaciones — {Reclamo|Queja} #{id}: {name}"

## Rate Limiting

This endpoint is protected by rate limiting:
- **Limit:** 50 requests per 15-minute window per IP address
- **Response when exceeded:** `429 Too Many Requests`

```json
{
  "error": "Demasiadas solicitudes, intenta de nuevo más tarde."
}
```
